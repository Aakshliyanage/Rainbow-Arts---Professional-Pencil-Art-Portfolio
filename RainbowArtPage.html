About Me

Bringing Your Vision to Life with Pencil and Paper


I'm Aakash Lyanage. With over 10 years of experience in pencil art, I specialize in creating stunning, hyper-realistic portraits and illustrations that capture the unique personality and emotions of my subjects.

Each piece is meticulously hand-drawn using only the highest quality materials, ensuring your artwork will stand the test of time and become a cherished family heirloom.

My passion lies in transforming your precious photographs into beautiful, one-of-a-kind works of art that tell a story and evoke emotion.
